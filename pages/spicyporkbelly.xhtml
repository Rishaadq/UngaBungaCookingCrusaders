Want something with a kick and flavor? Try this Korean Spicy Pork Belly Stir Fry

Ever got tired of eating junk food everyday, so you try to order delivery but think the delivery fee is a waste of money? This Korean spicy pork belly is called JaeYukBokkeum, and it is full of flavor with tangy sauce that will satisfy both your tongue and stomach!

The Directions

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 Minutes 30 Minutes 45 Minutes
  • One whole garlic
  • Pork belly
  • One onion
  • Two green onion
  • Two spoons of Gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • Two spoons of Gochugaru (Korean red pepper powder)
  • Two spoons of Soy Sauce
  • Two spoons of Maple syrup or honey
  • Two spoons of Sugar

Steps to Cook

  1. Mince or slice the garlics, however you want. Slice the onion and green onions, and slice the pork belly to one bite sizes. (Don't cut it too thick otherwise the seasoning won't go in thoroughly!)
  2. On to the sauce. Mix all seasonings together except sugar.
  3. Saute the meat on a round pan on high heat. When the meat starts browning, add sugar to coat the meat with sweetness.
  4. When meat seems to be ready, add the veggies and sauce together and start stir frying altogether.
  5. When you think the veggies softened enough and everything has mixed well, it is ready!
