How to Make Sirloin Steak: Not even Terry Crews will be able to hold a candle to you!

You learn how to cook a meidum rare sirloin steak to perfection, and you too, will be able to beat Terry Crews in an arm wrestle.



Now.... here's the recipe.

The Glorious Ingredients

Prep Time

5 minutes

Cook Time

10 minutes

Total Time

15 minutes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning
  • 1 10 ounce sirloin steak
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1 sprig thyme for garnish

Steps to cook

  1. Take the sirloin steak out and generously season with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Put the stove on medium high heat.
  3. In a skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter, and place the minced garlic and thyme in the hot liquid butter. Mix a little bit.
  4. Place the seasoned sirloin steak on the skillet. Flatten out the steak with a spatula so every part of the side of the steak can be cooked. Wait 2 minutes.
  5. Flip the steak over. Cook for two minutes. By this time, the fragrance of the steak should make your mouth water.
  6. And you're done! You have a ton of protein and you can make it all in 15 minutes. Eat this on a regular basis, and you too, will be able to become the arm-wrestling world champion.
