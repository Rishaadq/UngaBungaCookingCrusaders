Crunchy and juicy: Chicken Quesadilla

This is for you Mexican food lovers! This quesadilla is crunchy unlike a Burrito, and its got warm fillings inside thats juicy and tender.

The Directions

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

25 minutes

Total Time

35 minutes
  • Any cooking oil
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • Chicken breast
  • Oregano, Cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Cheddar cheese AND Monterey Jack cheese
  • Sour cream, Salsa, Guacamole if desired

Steps to Cook

  1. Thinly slice chicken breast and grill on medium-high heat for 5 minutes. Season with all the seasonings listed above.
  2. Cook onions and peppers in the same pan and season them lightly with each seasonings. Cook until softened. Then move everything to plate.
  3. Add 1 tortilla sheet with heavy sprinkle of each cheese, then add the chicken and veggie mixture. Fold the tortilla in half.
  4. Cook each side of the tortilla for about 3 mins. for that crunchy, brownish side.
